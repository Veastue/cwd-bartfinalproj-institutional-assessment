Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I change my password and/or Secure PIN?

    You can change your password and Secure PIN by following the steps below:

    Updating your Password

    1. Log in to Apostolos app

    2. Go to Me page and click Account Security

    3. Click Update Login Password

    4. Create a new password and click Submit

    5. Receive a confirmation that your password has been successfully updated

    Updating your PIN

    1. Log in to Apostolos app

    2. Go to Me page and click Account Security

    3. Click Update PIN

    4. Enter your old Secure PIN

    5. Input and confirm your new 6-digit Secure PIN

    6. Receive a confirmation that your Secure PIN has been successfully updated

    Note: If you have encountered issues logging in to your Apostolos app, please call our Customer Service at (+632) 8891 7000.

  • What are the applicable rates, fees and limits for my Apostolos account?
